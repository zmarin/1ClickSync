Zoho connection

Connect or reconnect Zoho to unlock live discovery

This step determines which services can move from read-only discovery into managed actions. Reconnect when a service card says more scopes are required.

If Mail or another service needs extra OAuth scopes, reconnecting refreshes the Zoho consent grant.
