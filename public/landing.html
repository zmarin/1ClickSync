How It Works

Three steps. Every site covered.

60 seconds per site. Your apps stop being pet projects and start building your pipeline.

01

Connect Your CRM

One OAuth click. We detect your datacenter, encrypt your tokens, and you're in. No API keys, no config files.

02

Build Forms for Each Site

Pick a target module (Leads, Contacts, or Deals), choose a Lead Source per site so you know where every inquiry came from, and we generate the embed code.

03

Paste & Pipeline

Drop the snippet on your site. Someone fills it out — they appear in your CRM pipeline. Process them like a real business, not a side project.
